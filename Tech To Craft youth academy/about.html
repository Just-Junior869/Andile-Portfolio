Our Story

Founded in 2020, Tech2Craft Youth Academy emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between traditional crafts and modern technology, creating opportunities for youth to thrive in both worlds.

Our Mission

To empower youth through practical skills education, combining traditional crafts with modern technology, creating well-rounded professionals ready for the future workforce.

Our Vision

To be the leading youth development institution that bridges traditional craftsmanship with technological innovation, creating a new generation of skilled entrepreneurs and professionals.
