Program Overview

Our Phone Repair program provides comprehensive training in mobile device repair and maintenance. Learn from experienced technicians and gain practical skills in diagnosing and fixing common smartphone issues.

What You'll Learn

Technical Skills

  • Hardware Diagnostics
  • Screen Replacement
  • Battery Service
  • Component Repair

Business Skills

  • Customer Service
  • Repair Estimation
  • Inventory Management
  • Shop Operations
