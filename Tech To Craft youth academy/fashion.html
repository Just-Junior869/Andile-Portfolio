Program Overview

Our Fashion Design program combines traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design principles. Learn from industry professionals and develop the skills needed to create sustainable, marketable fashion collections.

What You'll Learn

Design & Construction

  • Fashion Illustration
  • Pattern Making
  • Garment Construction
  • Textile Knowledge

Industry Skills

  • Trend Analysis
  • Sustainable Practices
  • Collection Development
  • Fashion Business
