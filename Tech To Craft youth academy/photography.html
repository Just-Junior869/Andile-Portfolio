Program Overview

Our Photography & Editing program combines technical camera skills with advanced digital editing techniques. Learn from professional photographers and develop the skills needed for a successful career in visual media.

What You'll Learn

Photography Skills

  • Camera Operations
  • Composition Techniques
  • Lighting Setup
  • Studio Photography

Digital Editing

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Lightroom Workflow
  • Color Correction
  • Digital Retouching
