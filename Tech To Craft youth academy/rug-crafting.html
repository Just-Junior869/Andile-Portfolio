Program Overview

Our Rug Crafting program combines traditional techniques with modern design principles to create unique, marketable pieces. Learn from experienced artisans and develop both creative and business skills.

What You'll Learn

Design & Technique

  • Pattern Design
  • Color Theory
  • Traditional Methods
  • Modern Techniques

Business Skills

  • Material Sourcing
  • Pricing Strategy
  • Marketing Basics
  • Online Sales

Curriculum

  • Introduction to Tools and Materials
  • Basic Stitching Techniques
  • Color Theory and Design Principles

  • E-commerce Basics
