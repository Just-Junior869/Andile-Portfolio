About Me

Hi, I'm Andile Mabida, a 19-year-old frontend developer and UX designer based in Amanzimtoti, KwaZulu-Natal. I’m passionate about crafting beautiful, intuitive digital experiences that not only look good—but also feel good to use.

From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by the intersection of design and technology. What began as curiosity soon evolved into a purpose: creating visually striking interfaces that solve real-world problems. Whether I’m fine-tuning a layout or optimizing a user flow, I approach every project with focus, creativity, and a drive for excellence.

Where I’m From

I proudly call Amanzimtoti my home—a coastal town that has taught me the value of simplicity, clarity, and resilience. Growing up here, I developed an eye for detail and a mindset that embraces growth and learning.

What Drives Me

I believe in continuous learning and staying ahead in a fast-paced digital world. From mastering the latest CSS techniques to studying UX psychology, I'm always pushing myself to deliver better, cleaner, smarter work. I don't just build interfaces—I craft experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Beyond the Code

Outside of development, I have a keen interest in photography, which further sharpens my sense of composition and storytelling. I also love teaching and sharing knowledge—empowering others to discover their own creative potential.
